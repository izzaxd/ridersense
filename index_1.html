The Product

Two components. One seamless safety system.

RiderSense combines a forward-facing dashboard display with a rear sensing unit — so riders monitor what's behind them without ever turning their head.

Screen Display

Dashboard visual display

A visual display mounted on top of the built-in dashboard, letting riders monitor rear traffic without turning their head.

  • Real-time rear camera footage
  • Live distance indicator (e.g. "2m")
  • Keeps the rider's focus forward
Rear Camera & Sensor

Rear sensing unit

A combined camera and distance sensor that captures the area behind the rider, reducing blind spots and alerting them to approaching vehicles.

  • Wide rear-field blind-spot capture
  • Distance measurement of approaching traffic
  • Weatherproof, vibration-resistant casing
How it works

An integrated hardware + software system.

Real-time camera feed, on-device distance calculation, and visual proximity alerts on the dashboard — engineered to survive dense, urban riding conditions.

STEP 01

Capture

The rear unit captures live footage of the area behind the rider.

STEP 02

Calculate

The distance sensor measures how close approaching vehicles are.

STEP 03

Display

Footage and a live distance indicator appear on the dashboard display.

STEP 04

Alert

Visual proximity alerts warn the rider of vehicles in the rear blind spot.
